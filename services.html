Our AI Chatbot Capabilities

Fully customizable features to match your business needs

01

Intelligent FAQ & Response System

Your bot answers questions the way YOU want. You define what the bot should say for specific questions, and we build it exactly as you specify.

  • Customized Answers: Every response is tailored to your business needs
  • Multiple Platforms: Works on WhatsApp and Instagram simultaneously
  • Easy Updates: Change responses anytime without code changes
  • Smart Routing: Direct customers to the right department or information
02

Appointment Booking

Customers can book appointments directly through chat. Your database stays updated in real-time, and office hours automation prevents bookings outside business hours.

  • Real-Time Availability: Shows only available time slots
  • Office Hours Automation: No bookings outside your working hours
  • Day-Based Availability: Mark specific days as unavailable instantly
  • Custom Rules: Define your own booking logic per requirement
  • Auto Confirmations: Send booking confirmations to customers
03

Customer Information Capture

Automatically collect customer details like name, email, phone, requirements. All data is stored securely in your database for follow-up.

  • Flexible Fields: Capture exactly what you need from customers
  • Smart Validation: Ensure data quality before storage
  • Database Integration: Direct sync to your database
  • Organized Records: Review and manage leads in your system
  • Lead Scoring: Qualify leads based on custom criteria
04

Database Integration & Setup

We handle complete database setup and integration. Your bot syncs with your system, checks availability, and updates records in real-time.

  • Full Setup Included: We configure everything for you
  • Real-Time Sync: Data updates instantly
  • Availability Checking: Bot checks slots before confirming bookings
  • Custom Fields: Integrate with your existing data structure
  • Secure Integration: Your data is protected with proper authentication
05

Multi-Language Support

Communicate with customers in their language. Starter plan includes 1 language; Professional plan supports multiple languages including Hindi, English, Gujarati, Marathi, and more.

  • Starter Plan: 1 language of your choice
  • Professional Plan: All languages supported
  • Auto-Detection: Bot detects customer language automatically
  • Professional Translations: All responses professionally translated
  • Easy Switching: Add/change languages anytime
06

Image Sending Capability

Your bot can send images, menus, catalogs, product pictures - giving professional visual presentation of your offerings. (Professional plan feature)

  • Professional Presentation: Send product images, menus, catalogs
  • Branded Content: Showcase your business professionally
  • Rich Media: Engage customers with visual content
  • Easy Updates: Change images without bot reconfiguration
  • Multiple Formats: Supports JPG, PNG, and other formats
07

Human-like Conversations

Your bot feels like chatting with a real person. Typing indicators, seen/read receipts, blue ticks - all the features that make conversations feel natural.

  • Typing Indicators: Shows "typing..." like a real person
  • Seen Ticks: Displays when messages are read
  • Blue Ticks: Confirms message delivery
  • Natural Responses: Conversation flows naturally
  • No Spam Feel: Customers feel valued and heard
08

Complete Customization

Everything is customizable. Your bot greets with YOUR company name, responds with YOUR personality, and follows YOUR exact business rules.

  • Conversation Flows: Design exactly how the bot should talk
  • Company Branding: Bot greets with your company name
  • Voice & Personality: Bot reflects your brand voice
  • Custom Rules: Office hours, holiday management, special instructions
  • Professional Appearance: Fully branded experience for your customers

Passive Responses Only

Your bot never sends unsolicited messages

Our chatbot is passive by design. It only responds when customers reach out first. Your bot will NEVER send unsolicited messages, guaranteeing a professional customer experience without being intrusive or spammy.
