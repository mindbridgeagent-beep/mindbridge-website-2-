How We Work

A transparent, 4-step process to build your perfect chatbot

01

Understanding (Days 1-2)

We dive deep into your business to understand your needs and opportunities.

What Happens:

  • Analyze your business requirements and customer journey
  • Understand your pain points and automation opportunities
  • Discuss your goals and expectations
  • Identify what the bot should do and say
  • Review your target audience and use cases

Outcome:

A clear brief of what your bot needs to accomplish and how it should behave.

02

Design (Days 3-5)

We map out the perfect conversation flows and bot behavior.

What Happens:

  • Design conversation flows based on customer interactions
  • Define all response templates and messaging
  • Plan database integration points
  • Map out office hours and automation rules
  • Plan appointment booking flow and validation
  • Create customer information capture fields

Outcome:

You review and approve the complete bot design before we start building.

03

Build & Integration (Days 6-13)

We develop your bot and integrate it with your systems.

What Happens:

  • Develop the bot with all approved features
  • Integrate with WhatsApp Business API and/or Instagram
  • Connect to your database (we set this up)
  • Configure automation rules and office hours
  • Set up language support as needed
  • Implement image sending and other features
  • Rigorous testing and quality assurance

Outcome:

A fully functional bot that's been tested and optimized.

04

Deploy & Support (Day 14-15 & Ongoing)

We launch your bot and provide ongoing support to optimize results.

What Happens:

  • Final setup and configuration
  • Bot goes live on your WhatsApp and Instagram
  • Monitor performance in first days
  • Make any final tweaks based on real conversations
  • Ongoing support throughout your subscription
  • Regular optimization for better results

Outcome:

Your bot is live, working 24/7, with our team supporting you as needed.
